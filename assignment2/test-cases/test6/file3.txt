The irony of the situation had not escaped her.
She had taken years to sculpt the perfect persona with the perfect look that she shared on Instagram.
She knew her hundreds of thousands of followers envied that life she showed and stayed engaged with her because they wanted that life too.
The truth was that she wanted the perfect life she portrayed more than any of her fans.
The fact was that despite all the perfection she shared on social media, her life was actually more of a mess than most.
